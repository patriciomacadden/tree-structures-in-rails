# Typography Hierarchy

### H3: Labels and Categories
Use H3 for labels and categories (appears in Sinaptia blue)

#### H4: Smaller Headers
Use H4 for sub-labels and smaller headers

Regular paragraph text uses the Inter font at 18px with light weight for easy reading.

- Bullet points for lists
- Multiple items in sequence
- Clean and organized

`Inline code` for technical terms or commands.
