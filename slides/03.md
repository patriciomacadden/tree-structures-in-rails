# Writing Slides

## Frontmatter Options

Add options at the top of your markdown file:

```yaml
---
variant: center    # "top" (default) or "center"
mode: dark         # "dark" (default) or "light"
---
```

## Variant: Top
Default layout with content starting at the top (perfect for content-heavy slides)

## Variant: Center
Centers content vertically (ideal for title slides or minimal content)
